About us:

Founded in 1987 and later defunked, Burple is now revived as a fruity, crushed-ice
beverage. Burple is among some of the highest performing athlete's favourite drinks for
cooling down during practices. Our sought after beverage is in the ranks of famous drinks
like "Slushy" and "Kool-Aid".
